Frequently Asked Questions
How long is each session? ▼
Most sessions are 60 minutes. This includes time for the healing modality, guidance, and any questions you may have. Some sessions may be shorter or longer depending on your needs.
What if I'm new to healing work? ▼
Perfect! Sasha works with people at all levels of spiritual experience. She will explain everything and create a comfortable, safe space for your journey. No prior knowledge is needed.
Can I do sessions online or only in-person? ▼
Sessions are available online via video call. Energy work is just as effective remotely as it is in-person. You'll be guided through the process and can join from the comfort of your space.
How often should I book sessions? ▼
This depends on your goals and needs. Some people benefit from weekly sessions, while others prefer monthly. Sasha will recommend a schedule based on your situation during your first session.
What should I prepare for a session? ▼
Simply find a quiet, comfortable space where you won't be disturbed. Wear comfortable clothing, have water nearby, and set an intention for what you'd like to explore or heal during the session.
What is your cancellation policy? ▼
Please provide at least 48 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. Cancellations made with less notice may incur a fee. Contact Sasha directly to discuss your circumstances.
